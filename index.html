From the Hon. Head

Best Chapter award winner repeatedly for several years, Mumbai Chapter is the acclaimed leader among all Chapters of the Computer Society of India. Situated centrally at Mumbaiâ€™s MIDC area, this Chapter is the epicentre of a host of activities conducted for its members and the IT community at large.
With over 100 technical and non-technical professional courses conducted at its in-house training centre, CSI-Mumbai is focused on organising career enhancement and knowledge sharing activities among IT professionals.
Besides professional training and workshops,the Chapter conducts the following on a regular basis: Software Improvement Network Programme (SPIN) Security Technology Forum (STF) CXO Meet, CSI Round Table National and International conferences and seminars.
