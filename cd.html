Chairman's Desk

Dear Members and Colleagues,
The Real Seed for The Computer society of India (CSI) was first sown in the year 1965 with a handful of Technology savvy of IT Enthusiasts, who were a computer user group and would wanted to share their valuable knowledge & exchange their unique Innovative Disruptive Technology Ideas on the fast emerging Dynamic Market Multiple Sectors.

Now, today the CSI takes pride in being the largest & most valuable & Professionally managed body of and for IT professionals in India. The Real Value PURPOSE of CSI Scientific & Educational is directed towards the advancement of the Invented theory and Incubated diversified practice of unique Technology in computer science and IT.
As a Computer service distributed as many BRANCHES all over the country. Currently having more than 500 student branches & rooted firmly at 73 chapters in different locations & activity centre’s including smaller towns and cities of the country. The idea is to spread the unique latest upgraded updated Computer Business Information Technology Secured Solutions knowledge, and provide opportunities to as many interested as possible.

CSI - Computer society of India as an Organisation has grown to an enviable size of 100,000 strong CSI Members consisting of diversified, Multi-Talented, Multi-lingual Multi-Sector Relevant Knowledgeable, Industry specific, Government, Expertise and special Experts from Academia Professionals with varied backgrounds like, Academicians, Retired Government staffs, Scientists, Researchers, CIO’s, CTO’s, Security Experts & Vendors, Software Developers & Vendors, Project managers & IT Vendors etc.

We have Large pool of experienced Resources & Practitioner Trainers, Architects throughout its membership across the country. An active Digital Library preserves all the Digital Assets of CSI. Opportunity to participate in the international, national, regional and chapter events of CSI such as conferences, seminars, symposia, workshops, technical talks, free lectures on latest technological advancements in IT at discounted rates between 10%-50%. CSI Other Important Chapter activities which offers a platform for networking with distinguished industry leaders, scientists, academicians include, CSI-SPIN Knowledge Forum Activities, Security Forum Activities, Symposia, CIO Meets, Annual CIO Summit, Start Up Meets. Opportunity to offer workshops/ trainings in collaboration with CSI.

Join Special Interest Group (SIG) for research, promotion, and dissemination activities for selected domains, both established and emerging every year. Opportunity to deliver guest lecturers in educational institutes associated to CSI across India. Right to vote in CSI elections. Opportunity to become part of CSI management Committee at Chapter, Regional and National levels subject to the eligibility criteria. CSI also works towards a global approach, by seeking out alliances with organizations overseas willing to come forward & participate in such activities. CSI represents the Indian community in all the technical Committees & some of the working groups of the International Federation of Information Processing (IFIP) since 1974. With all due Respect, having Faith Peace on Trusted Truth, looking for a Nice & wonderful Human being, who can show me “A drop of knowledge”, that can add up my life with a Purpose & a Meaning to live long love life.
- Jai Hind.


Dr. Vinay Kore.
Chairman, CSI TKIET Chapter
